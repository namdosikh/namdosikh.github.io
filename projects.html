Secret Sharing Scheme via Skew Polynomial

MA4094 Final Project (August 2023 - July 2024)

Explored the application of a specific non-commutative ring, known as the Skew Polynomial Ring, in a public key cryptography scheme called the Shamir Secret Sharing Scheme. The focus is on the complexity of Skew Polynomials Interpolation, which can enhance the security of the scheme.

Perancangan Rute Terpendek Pengantaran Paket di Kampus ITB Ganesha

MA3051 Elementary Graph Theory (November 2023 - December 2023)

Optimized package delivery routes at ITB Ganesha campus using Dijkstra's algorithm with a team, modeling the campus as a weighted graph, considering one-way traffic rules and building access to minimize travel distance and improve logistics efficiency.

Sistem Deteksi Dini Demam Berdarah Dengue Kota Bandung

MA3271 Mathematical Modelling (March 2023 - May 2023)

Developed an early warning system for Demam Berdarah Dengue (DBD) outbreaks in Bandung, modeling climate-related factors with a team using Vector Autoregressive and Binomial Negative models with R for analysis and Python for visualization, to analyze the relationship between climate and DBD cases.
